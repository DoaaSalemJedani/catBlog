3 reasons to own a cat

Published:
10/10/2022
Author:
Doaa Salem
Information Technology Student at KAU

You will be saving a life!

Every year, there are usually somewhere around a million cats euthanized in shelters each year! If you adopt a cat from a shelter, you will be directly saving a cat's life.


Soft Kitty, warm kitty

Cats are very nice to hug. Some studies even report a soft, warm kitty can help you sleep better.


They are funny

Cats can provide hours of entertainment with their silly antics. What is more fun than watching a cat trying to catch the red dot of a laser pointer? Or watching a cat play with a jingling ball?

“Every cat is my best friend.”
