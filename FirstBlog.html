Odor Management For Cats

Living in an apartment may mean that you have less space and might have to cope with the lack of ventilation in your apartment. To minimize unpleasant odors in your apartment, pick the right kitty litter. Be sure to clean the litter box every day to ensure odors don't fester. In addition to the litter box, pay special attention to your cat's food bowl and food area. Regularly cleaning around where your cat eats will help to prevent food odors.
